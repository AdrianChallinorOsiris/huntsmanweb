A community meeting took place on 15 January 2024 to discuss the proposed development at The Huntsman Sports Ground and Moorehead Way. Residents raised concerns about traffic, infrastructure, and the impact on local amenities.

## Key points discussed

- Overview of the planning application
- Timeline for objections
- How to submit letters to the planning authority
- Next steps for the community group
